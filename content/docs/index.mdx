# Welcome to LockedIn

LockedIn is a social status sharing application that lets you communicate when you're "locked in" and focused on work. Share your status with friends and see who else is in the zone.

## What is LockedIn?

LockedIn helps you:
- **Share your status** - Let friends know when you're locked in and working
- **Stay connected** - See who else is focused and what they're working on
- **Build accountability** - Publicly commit to your work sessions

## Quick Start

1. **Sign in** with your Twitter/X or GitHub account
2. **Set your status** - Toggle between "Locked In" and "Not Locked In"
3. **Add a message** - Optionally share what you're working on (up to 280 characters)
4. **Find friends** - Search for other users and see their status
